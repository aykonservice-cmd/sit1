About Our Yeshiva

Rooted in Tradition, Committed to Excellence

For decades, Derech Ayson has stood as a beacon of Torah learning in the Novardok tradition. Our intensive Talmudic studies program develops scholars with a lifelong commitment to Jewish law, ethics, and leadership.

Founded by Rabbi Yechiel Yitzchok Perr and Rabbi Nachman Bulman, our yeshiva continues to nurture the next generation of Torah leaders under the guidance of Rabbi Moshe Perr.

Our Programs

Pathways of Learning

Beis Medrash

Intensive Talmudic study for post-high school students. Our comprehensive curriculum builds strong analytical skills and deep Torah knowledge.

  • Talmud Intensive (5 credits/term)
  • Talmud Research (5 credits/term)
  • Talmud Survey (2 credits/term)

Kollel Ner Rochel Leah

Advanced learning program for married students, providing continued growth in Torah scholarship and preparation for community leadership.

  • Advanced Talmud Study
  • Halacha Seminars
  • Community Leadership Training

Musar & Ethics

Character development in the Novardok tradition, featuring the Rosh Yeshiva's renowned ethics lectures and personal growth guidance.

  • Weekly Musar Shiurim
  • Personal Guidance (Hashkafa)
  • Character Development Workshops

Begin Your Journey

Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Familiarity with Aramaic and Hebrew
  • Completion of 150 folio pages of Talmud
  • Competence in Pentateuch and Commentaries
  • Commitment to Torah observance

Application Process

  • Submit application by May 15th
  • Provide high school records
  • Include letters of recommendation
  • Oral examination and interview
  • Decision within one month

Financial Information

  • Tuition: $10,400 per year
  • Room & Board: $10,920 per year
  • 67% of students receive financial aid
  • Institutional scholarships available
